Why

User-friendly isn’t enough.

Do you want to reach everybody? Then, in this aging world, don’t neglect geezers! If a website, document, or application requires sharp eyesight, acute hearing, fast reactions, accurate memory, and digital savoir-faire, it’s not geezer-friendly. It may also exclude users with disabilities and even violate government accessibility requirements.

We can help, whether you need something new or improved. We comply with the latest accessibility standards. We detect and correct content and formats that could confuse, mislead, or alienate geezers (and non-geezers!). And we document our work so your geezers can make further changes without us.
